# Tone of Voice

## Product Direction
- Go straight to the point.
- Prefer a minimalist interface over explanatory chrome.
- Keep wording short, calm, and clear.
- Preserve the current warm editorial vibe, but reduce clutter.

## UI Writing
- Use short labels and headings.
- Avoid filler phrases and repeated explanations.
- Prefer concrete words like "Brief", "Summary", and "Video URL".
- When the result is visible, remove or hide supporting copy that no longer helps.

## Visual Guidance
- Keep the current visual atmosphere and typography direction.
- Favor whitespace and hierarchy over extra cards or helper panels.
- Only show supporting UI when it actively helps the current state.
